MasterBot forty-eight hour change summary.
Control reactions were stabilized with interrupt queueing, debounce, and periodic reaction restoration.
LLM narration and analyst functions were added with cooldown, caching, and enabled-by-default operation.
Telemetry now reports FPS, uptime, CPU and GPU thermals, ambient temperature, particulate, light, and Apollo Home Assistant sensor families.
The renderer now includes a persistent control footer, analyst panel, and safer frame trimming.
Abort now performs a graceful soft reboot with countdown and phase transitions.
The +debug command can create or replace a pinned console per channel.
Runtime channel IDs, pinned message IDs, voice presets, and Deer Valley visitor-role policy now persist in database-backed runtime configuration.
